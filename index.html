The Problem

If you own mineral rights, you're probably losing money and don't know it.

I.

Wells quietly go dark

Operators stop paying on a well and never send a notice. Unless someone is reconciling every stub, you'd never know.

II.

Deductions creep up

Post-production deductions inch higher month after month. No announcement, no explanation - just a smaller check.

III.

Stubs are a mess

Every operator formats stubs differently. The numbers that matter are inconsistent, confusing, and buried in PDFs.

IV.

Nobody's watching

Most owners have no way to catch any of it - and neither does their CPA until tax season, when the money's already gone.
