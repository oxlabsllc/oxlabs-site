Mineral Owner & CPA Pain Points

Operator stubs don't reconcile themselves. And tax season arrives fast.

I.

Wells stop paying silently

Operators abandon wells or cut production with no notice. Most owners don't discover the missing payments until year-end tax prep.

II.

Post-production deductions creep

Processing, transportation, treating charges inch higher monthly. No explanation. Your net revenue shrinks without a word from the operator.

III.

Stubs are inconsistent

EOG formats differently than Pioneer. Production volumes, deduction dates, and payment terms vary by operator. Excel nightmares for CPAs.

IV.

Tax time is chaos

CPAs get shoeboxes of PDFs in December. Owners scramble to verify 1099s against stubs. No time to dispute errors. Just file and hope.

The Process

Operator stubs to tax-ready reports in four steps.

01

Forward your stubs

Email or upload monthly operator statements - PDFs, scans, images. From any operator: EOG, Pioneer, Diamondback, Cabot, BP, or regional E&P.

02

We parse and extract

OCR reads every line: well identifier, location, BOE produced, price, post-production deductions, net payment, operator name, payment date.

03

Alerts on errors

Flags underpayments, missing wells, deduction spikes, decimal errors, volume discrepancies - anything that changes from prior month.

04

Tax-ready output

Dashboard for owners. For CPAs: aggregated files ready for 1099 reconciliation, K-1 schedules, Schedule C, or client portal integration.
